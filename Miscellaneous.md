## What is Business Logic
Business logic is what  the system does, the general steps need to complete a business object. For  Example - Creating a  customer order 
i. Putting  item into  shopping  cart 
ii. add  a delivery address 
iii. Pay for  the order
iv. Create a receipt for the customer order


The monetary value is a notable figure, but no, I didn't see any of that or even get a bonus. My reward was knowing I'd done my job well and improved things for the people using the software I was responsible for.  
I enjoyed the satisfaction of solving a complex technical problem (how to make the process take less time) that also contributed to a positive benefit for the people using the software.


This is the essence of systems thinking:  
a small feature is never just a small feature.  
It is part of a larger architecture with many moving parts.
